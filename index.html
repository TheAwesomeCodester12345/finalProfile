The National Chinese Speech Contest (NCSC) is an annual competition organized by the American Academy of International Culture and Education (AAICE) in collaboration with the Chinese Language Teachers’ Association (CLTA). It invites middle and high school students across the U.S. to deliver speeches in Mandarin, aiming to motivate learners and provide a platform to showcase their language skills and cultural understanding.
